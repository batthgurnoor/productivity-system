# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

I want this project to be open, useful, and safe for everyone who contributes.

By participating, you agree to help keep discussions respectful and constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in language and tone.
- Assume good intent first.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Be patient with different experience levels.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or repeated hostility.
- Public or private abuse of any kind.
- Publishing someone’s private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies across project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels related to this repo).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, open a private security report if possible, or create an issue asking for maintainer attention.

I’ll review reports in good faith and respond as quickly as I can.

## Enforcement

I may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.

The goal is not punishment for its own sake—the goal is a healthy project environment where people can contribute.

---

This document is intentionally simple and direct. If the project grows, I may expand this policy.
